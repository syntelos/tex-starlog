Last month it rained ten inches.  The clouds cooled the atmosphere at his altitude of three hundred meters above the planned tidal mean.  When they cleared it was fifteen degrees.  A typical thunderstorm lowered the ambient temperature by five degrees.


Stuart’s farm covered the two miles of flood plan at its greatest extent.  The hills rose to fifty meters.  Covered in grass, the plain sprouted wild flowers, worms and wasps.  Cornelius readied for trees and bats and birds.  This quarter he would introduce dragonflies, ahead of the birds.
