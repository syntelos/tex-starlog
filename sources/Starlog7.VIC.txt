Shevek woke and opened his eyes.  Takver had turned sour.  He felt her ascend away.  He thought about a fast camera droid that could cut his time to seeing her again.  He wanted to see her, and thought the digital link image would be depressing.  He wrote to NAV. “Would like to see you”.  He waited for a reply.  None came.  The new differential analyzer called his name.  He needed a different kind of project to break his stride.  Or a nap.  The simpler of the two choices won.
